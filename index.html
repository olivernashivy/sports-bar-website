Our Story

Where Fans Come
Together

Arena 90 Sports Bar is a place where fans gather to watch the biggest games in a lively atmosphere. We combine great food, cold drinks, and high-definition screens to create the perfect sports experience.

Whether you're here for the championship final, a midweek fixture, or just to enjoy a cold one after work — Arena 90 is your home away from home. Our passion for sport and hospitality is woven into every part of the experience.
